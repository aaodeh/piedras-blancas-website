About Us

Excellence in Concrete Since 1995

Piedras Blancas Construction (White Rocks Construction) has been delivering exceptional concrete services for over 25 years. Specializing in residential and commercial concrete work, we take pride in creating durable, beautiful concrete structures that stand the test of time.

From decorative patios to heavy-duty industrial slabs, our experienced team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver superior results. We use only the highest quality materials and latest equipment to ensure every project meets our rigorous standards.

Whether you need a simple sidewalk or complex foundation work, we have the expertise to handle projects of all sizes with precision and professionalism.
